[h1] This is a Chinese-translation Mod for Infernal Eclipse of Ragnarok and other related mods [/h1]
You do not need this if you don't want to play IEoR in Chinese :D

[h1] =Introduction= [/h1]
Infernal Eclipse of Ragnarok already has built-in Chinese support.
This mod aims to solve the issue that localization updates with IEoR's update, thus translation bug fixing may not be timely.
[url=https://github.com/De1tA5/IEoR_CN] GitHub Repository [/url]

For some of the hard-coded texts, we use[url=https://github.com/TigerChenzzz/TerrariaTigerForceLocalizationLib] TigerForceLocalizationLib [/url] to translate them
